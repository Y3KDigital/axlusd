# INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS (INSTITUTIONAL)

**Asset:** Rough Chrysoberyl (Alexandrite), 2kg, Seal ID 00289944  
**Appraised Value:** USD $42,000,000  
**Purpose:** Define minimum insurance standards for custody, transit, and financing

---

## I. REQUIRED COVERAGES

### 1) All-Risk Property / Specie Coverage (Vault)
**Coverage Type:** Specie / High-Value Property ("all risk")

**Minimum Limit:** USD $42,000,000 (full appraised value)  
**Recommended Limit:** USD $50,000,000 (buffer for fees, transit, and dispute costs)

**Perils Covered (minimum):**
- Theft (inside and outside premises)
- Robbery (armed and unarmed)
- Fire, smoke, water damage
- Natural catastrophe (flood, earthquake, hurricane)
- Mysterious disappearance (preferably included)
- Employee dishonesty (fidelity)

**Exclusions to Avoid / Negotiate:**
- Unreasonable sublimits for gemstones
- Exclusions for “unattended” transit without defining procedures
- Exclusions that void coverage upon routine inspections

---

### 2) Transit Insurance (Door-to-Door)
Required for any movement of the Asset (Brazil export, vault deposit, appraisal inspection, lender inspection, sale).

**Coverage Type:** Door-to-door transit specie insurance

**Minimum Limit:** USD $42,000,000  
**Policy Must Cover:**
- Air transport
- Armored ground transport
- Customs clearance delays
- Temporary storage during transit

**Transit Providers:**
- Brinks Global Services
- Malca-Amit logistics
- Loomis (where applicable)

**Requirement:** Transit policy must be bound **before** any movement is initiated.

---

### 3) Third-Party Liability (Optional)
If inspections occur, consider third-party liability for:
- Damage during handling by appraiser or auditor
- Claims arising from inspection events

---

## II. INSURED PARTY, LOSS PAYEE, AND BENEFICIARY

### Baseline (No Financing)
**Named Insured:** SPV (recommended) or legal owner entity

### With Credit Facility
**Named Insured:** SPV  
**Loss Payee:** Lender (first priority)  
**Additional Insured (optional):** Custodian (for liability allocation)

**Required Clause:** Loss payable endorsement with lender as first loss payee.

---

## III. POLICY TERMS

### Deductible
**Target Deductible:** $10,000 – $100,000

Avoid deductibles that impair lender comfort (> $250,000) unless justified by premium savings.

### Valuation Basis
**Preferred Basis:** Agreed value / scheduled item value  
**Alternative:** Actual cash value (not preferred for gemstones)

### Territorial Scope
**Required:** Worldwide coverage (especially if vault is outside US)

### Cancellation Notice
**Minimum:** 30 days prior notice to SPV and lender  
**Preferred:** 60-90 days prior notice

---

## IV. INSURER QUALITY REQUIREMENTS

**Insurer Rating:** A- or higher (A.M. Best)  
**Reinsurers:** Named, reputable syndicates acceptable

**Broker Recommendation:** Use a broker experienced in:
- Fine art/specie
- Diamonds/gemstones
- High-value collectibles

---

## V. REQUIRED DOCUMENTS (DELIVERABLES)

Before any lender funding or token distribution:

- Certificate of Insurance (COI)
- Full policy (including endorsements)
- Loss payee endorsement (if lender involved)
- Proof of premium payment
- Claims contact details

## VII. PRACTICAL PREMIUM EXPECTATIONS

**Indicative Annual Premium Range (specie):**
- 0.10% – 0.25% of insured value per annum

For $42M:
- Low end: ~$42,000/year
- High end: ~$105,000/year

Actual premium depends on:
- Custodian security rating
- Location (US vs Switzerland vs UAE)
- Deductible
- Transit frequency
