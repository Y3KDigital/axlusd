# VAULT RFP CHECKLIST (REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL)

**Purpose:** Standardize vault due diligence and ensure custody meets lender/investor requirements.

---

## I. RFP PACKAGE CONTENTS (WHAT YOU SEND)

Provide custodians with:

- Asset summary (non-sensitive):
  - Rough Alexandrite, 2kg, sealed parcel
  - Appraised value: $42,000,000
  - Intended use: secured lending / RWA issuance
- Required custody start date
- Expected inspection cadence (quarterly)
- Insurance requirement summary
- Request for sample custody agreement

---

## II. CUSTODIAN RESPONSE REQUIREMENTS

### A. Corporate & Regulatory
- [ ] Legal name of custodian entity
- [ ] Licenses and regulatory oversight
- [ ] SOC 2 Type II / ISAE 3402 report availability
- [ ] Financial stability / parent company details
- [ ] References (2-3 clients with comparable value assets)

### B. Vault & Security
- [ ] Vault locations available (city, address)
- [ ] Security standards (UL rating or equivalent)
- [ ] Access controls (biometric, dual control)
- [ ] CCTV retention period
- [ ] Disaster protection (fire suppression, flood mitigation)
- [ ] Segregation (allocated vs commingled storage)

### C. Receiving & Release Procedures
- [ ] Receiving process (seal verification, photos, logging)
- [ ] Release controls (who can authorize release)
- [ ] Time to release (standard vs expedited)
- [ ] Procedure for third-party inspections

### D. Chain-of-Custody & Reporting
- [ ] Quarterly confirmation letter capability
- [ ] Inventory reporting format and frequency
- [ ] Inspection log retention
- [ ] Ability to support third-party auditor inspections

### E. Insurance
- [ ] Custodian’s insurance coverage (aggregate and per-item)
- [ ] Ability to schedule items up to $42M
- [ ] COI sample and insurer ratings
- [ ] Whether client must bring separate insurance

### F. Fees
- [ ] Storage fees (annual % or fixed)
- [ ] Receiving fees
- [ ] Inspection fees
- [ ] Release/withdrawal fees
- [ ] Transit/logistics services pricing (if offered)

### G. Legal Terms (Key Clauses)
- [ ] Bailment language (client retains title)
- [ ] Limitation of liability terms
- [ ] Indemnification provisions
- [ ] Choice of law and jurisdiction
- [ ] Confidentiality terms
- [ ] Ability to execute a tri-party control agreement (SPV–Lender–Custodian)

---

## III. SCORING MATRIX (RECOMMENDED)

| Category | Weight | Notes |
|---------|--------|------|
| Security & controls | 35% | Must be Tier-1 for $42M asset |
| Insurance adequacy | 20% | Must support $42M scheduled value |
| Audit/reporting | 15% | SOC 2 / confirmations |
| Legal terms | 15% | Tri-party control readiness |
| Fees | 10% | Secondary to safety |
| Logistics | 5% | Nice-to-have |

---

## IV. RED FLAGS (AUTO-DISQUALIFY)

- Refuses to provide license evidence
- No meaningful insurance or low gem sublimits
- Cannot support segregated storage
- Weak release controls (single-person release)
- No audit/reporting capability
- Poor references or unclear ownership

---

## V. OUTPUT

Select 1 primary custodian + 1 backup custodian.

**Next step after selection:** execute custody agreement + (if financing) tri-party control agreement.
