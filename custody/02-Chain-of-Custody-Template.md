## INSPECTION PROTOCOLS

### Quarterly Verification (Minimum Standard)
**Frequency:** Every 90 days  
**Scope:**
- Visual inspection of seal integrity
- Photograph seal from multiple angles
- Verify serial number matches record (00289944 or successor)
- Confirm parcel remains in designated vault location
- Review custodian's inventory log for discrepancies

**Inspector:** May be custodian staff, SPV representative, or independent auditor  
**Deliverable:** Quarterly confirmation letter to SPV + lender (if applicable)

---

### Annual In-Depth Verification
**Frequency:** Every 12 months  
**Scope:**
- Everything in quarterly verification, plus:
- Appraiser reaffirmation of value (light review, not full re-appraisal)
- Insurance policy review and renewal
- Custodian agreement compliance check

**Inspector:** Independent gemologist or appraiser  
**Deliverable:** Annual verification report

---

## EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

### Seal Tampering Detected
**Immediate Actions:**
1. Photograph seal from all angles
2. Notify SPV management immediately
3. Notify custodian management and security
4. Notify lender (if asset is pledged)
5. Notify insurer within 24 hours
6. Engage independent appraiser for emergency inspection
7. File police report if theft or fraud suspected

**Documentation:** All communications, photos, reports filed in this register

---

### Natural Disaster or Vault Breach
**Immediate Actions:**
1. Verify asset physical status with custodian
2. Inspect for damage
3. Notify insurer
4. Arrange alternative custody if vault compromised
5. Update chain-of-custody register with incident details
