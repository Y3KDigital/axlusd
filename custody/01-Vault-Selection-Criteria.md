## I. NON-NEGOTIABLE REQUIREMENTS

### ‚úÖ Tier-1 Institutional Grade
Custodian must meet ALL of the following:

**1. Licensed & Regulated**
- Operating under banking, trust, or security vault license
- Subject to regulatory examination (US, Swiss, UK, or ADGM authority)
- Clean regulatory history (no major enforcement actions in past 5 years)

**2. Insurance Coverage**
- Minimum $100M aggregate vault insurance (preferably $500M+)
- All-risk coverage including theft, fire, natural disaster
- Named insurer with A.M. Best rating of A or higher
- Willingness to schedule high-value items up to $50M

**3. Physical Security Standards**
- UL-rated or equivalent vault (minimum Class 3)
- 24/7 armed security personnel
- Multi-factor access control (biometric + keycard + PIN)
- CCTV with 90-day retention minimum
- Seismic, fire suppression, and flood protection

**4. Chain-of-Custody Protocols**
- Written procedures for item receipt, storage, inspection, and release
- Two-person integrity (dual control) for all high-value items
- Tamper-evident packaging verification at each interaction
- Digital logging of all access events

**5. Audit & Reporting**
- Annual SOC 2 Type II report or equivalent (ISAE 3402)
- Quarterly inventory confirmations available to asset owners
- Willingness to accommodate third-party auditor inspections

4. Conduct reference calls

9. Negotiate any custom terms (e.g., quarterly physical inspections, release protocols)
10. Execute custody agreement
11. Arrange asset transfer logistics
