# IOU PROGRAM RUNBOOK (ALXUSD)

**Purpose:** Day-to-day operational controls for issuing and managing `ALXUSD`.

---

## I. ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

- Program Owner: SPV Manager
- Compliance Approver: Compliance Officer (or counsel designee)
- Signers: multisig quorum
- Recordkeeper: Program Administrator

---

## II. STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES

### A) New Holder Onboarding
1. Collect KYC and execute agreement
2. Sanctions screening
3. Holder sets trustline
4. Approver authorizes trustline
5. Issuer unfreezes trustline
6. Distribution account transfers ALXUSD
7. Record tx hashes

### B) Quarterly Controls
- obtain vault confirmation letter
- confirm insurance in force
- reconcile outstanding balances vs cap
- post XRPL custody memo (optional but recommended)

### C) Annual Controls
- appraisal reaffirmation/update
- insurance renewal and endorsements
- governance review of cap

---

## III. INCIDENT RESPONSE

### A) Suspected Fraud / Sanctions
- freeze trustline immediately
- notify legal/compliance
- evaluate clawback authority
- preserve logs and evidence

### B) Custodian Incident
- notify insurer and lender (if any)
- obtain incident report
- determine if relocation required
- update custody memo and chain-of-custody register

---

## IV. RECORDKEEPING

Maintain:
- holder register
- agreements index
- transaction hash ledger
- approvals log
- quarterly and annual reports
