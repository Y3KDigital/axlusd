# SPV FORMATION GUIDE

**Purpose:** Establish bankruptcy-remote legal entity to hold the gemstone asset

---

## I. JURISDICTIONAL SELECTION

### Recommended Jurisdictions (Ranked)

#### 1️⃣ Delaware LLC (United States)
**Advantages:**
- Established legal precedent
- Creditor-friendly bankruptcy remoteness
- Familiar to US banks and credit desks
- Low annual maintenance

**Documents Required:**
- Certificate of Formation
- Operating Agreement (single-purpose, bankruptcy-remote language)
- Registered Agent appointment

**Timeline:** 3-5 business days  
**Cost:** $500-$2,000 (formation + first year)

---

#### 2️⃣ ADGM SPV (Abu Dhabi Global Market)
**Advantages:**
- English common law jurisdiction in UAE
- No corporate tax (subject to ADGM rules)
- Growing RWA hub
- Credible for Middle East/Asia investors

**Documents Required:**
- Memorandum of Association
- Articles of Association
- Registered Office confirmation
- Beneficial ownership declaration

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks  
**Cost:** $5,000-$15,000 (formation + licensing)

---

#### 3️⃣ Swiss AG (Switzerland)
**Advantages:**
- Maximum institutional credibility
- Strong asset protection laws
- Ideal for European investors

**Documents Required:**
- Articles of Incorporation
- Notarized formation deed
- Registered office + auditor (for larger entities)

**Timeline:** 4-8 weeks  
**Cost:** $10,000-$25,000

---

#### 4️⃣ Bahamas DARE Entity
**Advantages:**
- Digital asset regulatory framework
- No capital gains tax
- Growing crypto-native RWA jurisdiction

**Documents Required:**
- Memorandum & Articles
- DARE registration with SCB (Securities Commission)
- AML/KYC compliance framework

**Timeline:** 6-12 weeks  
**Cost:** $15,000-$30,000 (including regulatory filings)

---

## II. SPV STRUCTURAL REQUIREMENTS

### Non-Negotiable Features

✅ **Single-Purpose Entity**  
SPV owns ONLY the gemstone asset (or gemstone + proceeds from financing)

✅ **Bankruptcy Remoteness**  
Operating agreement includes:
- Independent director requirement
- Limitation on activities
- Prohibition on voluntary bankruptcy
- Substantive consolidation protection

✅ **Limited Recourse**  
Creditors/investors have claims ONLY against SPV assets, not parent company

✅ **Separation from Owner**  
Clear legal transfer of asset from Investments Danath Inc. → SPV

---

## III. FORMATION DOCUMENTS CHECKLIST

### Document 1: Certificate of Formation / Articles
**Contains:**
- Entity name: "[Name] Asset Holdings LLC" (or similar)
- Registered office address
- Registered agent
- Purpose: "To hold, maintain, and manage specified real-world assets"

---

### Document 2: Operating Agreement (LLC) / Bylaws (Corp)
**Key Sections:**

#### A. Purpose & Limitations
```
The Company is formed for the sole purpose of:
(a) Acquiring and holding title to one (1) gemstone asset ("Alexandrite Asset")
(b) Entering into financing agreements secured by said asset
(c) Issuing digital representations of economic interest in said asset
(d) Engaging in activities reasonably incidental to the above

The Company shall NOT:
- Engage in any other business
- Incur debt unrelated to the Asset
- Guarantee obligations of affiliates
- File voluntary bankruptcy without unanimous consent
```

#### B. Management Structure
- Manager: Investments Danath Inc. (or designated manager)
- Independent Director (required for bankruptcy remoteness)
- No authority to commingle assets

#### C. Transfer Restrictions
- Membership interests are non-transferable without manager consent
- Restrictions survive even after tokenization

---

### Document 3: Asset Contribution Agreement
**Purpose:** Legal transfer of gemstone from owner to SPV

**Key Terms:**
- Transferor: Investments Danath Inc.
- Transferee: [SPV Name]
- Asset Description: 2kg Chrysoberyl (Alexandrite), Seal 00289944
- Consideration: Membership interest in SPV
- Representations: Asset is free of liens, encumbrances
- Custody obligations: Asset to be placed in institutional vault within 30 days

**Exhibits:**
- Appraisal report (attached)
- Photo documentation
- Seal verification

---

### Document 4: Board Resolution (Owner Entity)
**From:** Investments Danath Inc.  
**Purpose:** Authorize asset transfer and SPV formation

**Resolves:**
1. Formation of SPV is approved
2. Transfer of Alexandrite asset to SPV is authorized
3. [Named Officer] is authorized to execute all necessary documents
4. Asset may be used for financing, tokenization, or secured lending

---

### Document 5: No-Lien / No-Encumbrance Letter
**Issued by:** Investments Danath Inc.  
**Addressed to:** [SPV], [Custodian], [Lender/Token Issuer as applicable]

**Certifies:**
- Asset is owned free and clear
- No security interests, liens, or claims exist
- No litigation affecting ownership
- Authorized for transfer and financing use

---

### Document 6: Independent Director Appointment
**Purpose:** Ensure bankruptcy remoteness

**Role of Independent Director:**
- Must consent to any bankruptcy filing
- Cannot be affiliate of parent company
- Typical compensation: $2,500-$10,000/year

**Candidates:**
- Corporate service providers (CSC, CT Corporation)
- Law firms with fiduciary divisions
- Professional independent directors
