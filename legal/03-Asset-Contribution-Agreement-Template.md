## RECITALS

**A.** Transferor is the lawful owner of certain rough gemstone assets, specifically described as:
- **Type:** Rough Chrysoberyl (variety Alexandrite)
- **Weight:** 2 kilograms (approximately 10,000 carats)
- **Origin:** Bahia, Brazil
- **Seal ID:** 00289944
- **Appraised Value:** USD $42,000,000
- **Appraisal Date:** August 18, 2025
- **Appraiser:** Professor Norman Michael Rodi, G.G.

(collectively, the "Asset")

**B.** Transferor desires to contribute the Asset to Transferee for the purpose of facilitating financing, tokenization, or other institutional use of the Asset.

**C.** Transferee is a special purpose entity formed solely for the purpose of holding, maintaining, and managing the Asset.

**D.** In consideration for the Asset, Transferor shall receive 100% of the membership interests in Transferee.

---

## ARTICLE 1: CONTRIBUTION OF ASSET

**1.1 Transfer of Asset**  
Transferor hereby contributes, assigns, transfers, and conveys to Transferee all of Transferor's right, title, and interest in and to the Asset, free and clear of all liens, security interests, encumbrances, and adverse claims.

**1.2 Delivery**  
Physical delivery of the Asset shall occur within thirty (30) days of the date of this Agreement by transfer to an institutional custodian selected by Transferee (the "Custodian"). Until such transfer, Transferor shall hold the Asset in trust for the benefit of Transferee.

**1.3 Documentation**  
The Asset shall be delivered together with:
- Original or certified copy of the appraisal report
- Photographic documentation of Seal ID 00289944
- Any export or customs documentation required for lawful transfer
- Chain-of-custody documentation

---

## ARTICLE 2: CONSIDERATION

**2.1 Issuance of Membership Interests**  
In consideration for the Asset, Transferee hereby issues to Transferor 100% of the membership interests in Transferee (the "Membership Interests").

**2.2 Valuation**  
The parties agree that the Asset has a fair market value of USD $42,000,000 based on the appraisal report dated August 18, 2025, and that the Membership Interests shall have an initial book value equal to such amount.

---

## ARTICLE 3: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES OF TRANSFEROR

Transferor represents and warrants to Transferee as follows:

**3.1 Ownership**  
Transferor is the sole legal and beneficial owner of the Asset and has full right, power, and authority to transfer the Asset to Transferee.

**3.2 No Encumbrances**  
The Asset is free and clear of all liens, pledges, security interests, encumbrances, mortgages, charges, or other adverse claims of any nature.

**3.3 No Litigation**  
There is no litigation, arbitration, governmental investigation, or other proceeding pending or, to Transferor's knowledge, threatened, that affects or could affect the Asset or Transferor's ability to transfer it.

**3.4 Accuracy of Appraisal**  
The appraisal report dated August 18, 2025 is accurate and complete to the best of Transferor's knowledge, and Transferor has not withheld any material information from the appraiser.

**3.5 Authenticity**  
The Asset is genuine Chrysoberyl (variety Alexandrite) as described in the appraisal report. Seal ID 00289944 has not been tampered with or altered since application by the appraiser.

**3.6 Export Compliance**  
To Transferor's knowledge, the Asset may be lawfully exported from Brazil and imported to [jurisdiction of Custodian] subject to applicable customs and regulatory procedures. Transferor agrees to cooperate in obtaining any required export permits.

**3.7 Authority**  
Transferor's board of directors has duly authorized this Agreement and the transactions contemplated hereby.

Transferee represents and warrants to Transferor as follows:

**4.1 Organization**  
Transferee is a limited liability company duly organized and validly existing under the laws of [Delaware].

**4.2 Authority**  
Transferee has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement and issue the Membership Interests.

**4.3 Single Purpose**  
Transferee is a special purpose entity formed solely to hold, maintain, and manage the Asset and engage in activities reasonably incidental thereto.

## ARTICLE 5: COVENANTS

**5.1 Custody**  
Transferee shall, within thirty (30) days of the date of this Agreement, cause the Asset to be deposited with an institutional custodian of recognized standing (e.g., Brinks, Malca-Amit, Swiss Freeport, or equivalent).

**5.2 Insurance**  
Transferee shall maintain insurance on the Asset in an amount not less than USD $42,000,000, naming Transferee as the insured and loss payee.

**5.3 Inspection Rights**  
Transferor (as 100% member of Transferee) shall have the right to inspect the Asset or receive vault confirmations from the Custodian upon reasonable notice.

**5.4 Limitation on Activities**  
Transferee shall not engage in any business activities unrelated to the Asset or guarantee any obligations of Transferor or its affiliates.

## ARTICLE 6: CLOSING

**6.1 Closing Date**  
The closing of the transactions contemplated by this Agreement (the "Closing") shall occur on the date of this Agreement or such other date as the parties mutually agree.

**6.2 Closing Deliveries by Transferor**
At Closing, Transferor shall deliver:
- Physical possession of the Asset (or irrevocable instructions to transfer to Custodian)
- Certified copy of board resolution authorizing this Agreement
- No-lien letter in form acceptable to Transferee
- Original or certified copy of appraisal report
- Photo documentation and seal verification

**6.3 Closing Deliveries by Transferee**  
At Closing, Transferee shall deliver:
- Membership certificates representing 100% interest in Transferee
- Executed operating agreement of Transferee
- Custody agreement (or draft, to be finalized within 30 days)
