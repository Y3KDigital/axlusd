# IOU PROGRAM TERMS (ALXUSD) — INSTITUTIONAL SUMMARY

**Instrument:** XRPL Issued Asset (IOU)  
**Currency Code:** `ALXUSD`  
**Issuer:** SPV-controlled XRPL issuer account (the “Issuer”)  
**Program Cap:** USD $10,000,000 (maximum outstanding)  
**Collateral Reference:** Alexandrite gemstone (2kg), Seal ID 00289944, held in institutional custody

---

## I. PURPOSE AND CHARACTER

`ALXUSD` is a **ledger-native claim on the Issuer**, recorded on XRPL and governed by XRPL trustlines.

`ALXUSD` is intended for **permissioned institutional use**, including:
- Private credit facilities
- Settlement between known counterparties
- Trade finance and inventory finance bridging
- Treasury and intercompany settlement

`ALXUSD` is **not**:
- A retail stablecoin
- A public token offering
- A permissionless payment token
- A promise of daily liquidity

---

## II. ISSUER OBLIGATIONS

### 1) Nature of Claim
Holding `ALXUSD` constitutes a contractual claim against the Issuer, subject to:
- These terms
- Any executed subscription/loan/settlement agreements
- Applicable law

### 2) No Physical Redemption
`ALXUSD` is **not redeemable for physical delivery** of the gemstone.

All redemptions are **financial** (cash, net settlement, buyback, or successor instrument) as defined in the Redemption Policy.

### 3) Cap and Over-Issuance Prohibition
Issuer shall not allow total outstanding `ALXUSD` to exceed **USD $10,000,000**.

Outstanding amount includes all balances held by authorized holders on XRPL.

---

## III. COLLATERAL STATEMENT (DISCLOSURE)

The Issuer represents that `ALXUSD` is issued against a collateral base consisting of a gemstone asset described as:

- Rough Chrysoberyl (Alexandrite)
- Weight: 2kg (approx. 10,000 ct; ±5% tolerance)
- Seal ID: 00289944
- Appraised value: USD $42,000,000 (August 18, 2025)
- Custody: Institutional vault (to be specified in custody receipt)

This collateral statement is supported by:
- Appraisal report (PDF)
- Vault receipt
- Insurance certificate
- XRPL memo anchors (appraisal hash, IPFS CID, custody identifiers)

**Important:** Collateralization is a **risk mitigant**, not a guarantee of repayment in all cases.

---

## IV. PERMISSIONING AND TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

### 1) Trustline Gating
Only counterparties with approved trustlines may hold `ALXUSD`.

### 2) Authorization Policy
Issuer may require KYC/AML before authorizing trustlines. Authorization criteria include:
- Identity verification
- Sanctions screening
- Accredited/qualified investor status (if applicable)
- Contract execution (loan/subscription agreement)

### 3) Freezing and Clawback
Issuer reserves the right to:
- Freeze transfers and/or balances (XRPL issuer controls)
- Claw back balances where legally required or contractually permitted

All such powers must be disclosed to holders and exercised per written policy.

---

## V. REDEMPTION TERMS (OVERVIEW)

Redemption is governed by the Redemption Policy document.

Permitted redemption modes may include:
- Cash redemption at maturity
- Net settlement against obligations
- Buyback at par or agreed haircut
- Exchange into successor instrument (e.g., note, facility roll)

No holder shall have the right to demand physical delivery of the gemstone.

---

## VI. DEFAULTS AND REMEDIES (HIGH LEVEL)

### Defaults
A default may occur upon:
- Issuer insolvency
- Material breach of program covenants
- Loss of collateral without adequate insurance recovery
- Fraud or misrepresentation

### Remedies
Remedies are governed by off-chain legal agreements (credit agreement, pledge agreement, etc.). XRPL records are evidentiary and operational, not a substitute for contract enforcement.

---

## VII. DISCLOSURES

- `ALXUSD` value depends on Issuer creditworthiness and collateral controls.
- Gemstone collateral is illiquid and valuation is not guaranteed liquidation value.
- `ALXUSD` is intended for sophisticated parties only.
- Transfers are restricted; secondary market is not promised.

See risk disclosures in /legal/04-Risk-Register.md.
