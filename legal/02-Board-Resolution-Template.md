## UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS

**Date:** _________________, 2026

**Entity:** Investments Danath Inc. (the "Company")

---

### RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Company is the lawful owner of certain rough gemstone assets, specifically a 2-kilogram parcel of Chrysoberyl (variety Alexandrite) originating from Bahia, Brazil, bearing Seal ID 00289944, with an appraised value of USD $42,000,000 as of August 18, 2025 (the "Asset");

WHEREAS, the Board of Directors (the "Board") deems it in the best interest of the Company to utilize the Asset for financing, secured lending, or tokenization purposes;

WHEREAS, to facilitate such use, the Board has determined that the Asset should be contributed to a special purpose vehicle (the "SPV") to be formed for the sole purpose of holding, maintaining, and managing the Asset;

WHEREAS, the Board has reviewed the appraisal report prepared by Professor Norman Michael Rodi, G.G., and confirms the Asset is held free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, and adverse claims;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that:

---

## RESOLUTION 1: SPV FORMATION AUTHORIZED

The formation of a special purpose entity (the "SPV") under the laws of [Delaware / ADGM / Switzerland / Bahamas] is hereby approved and authorized.

The SPV shall be named: **[INSERT SPV NAME] Asset Holdings LLC** (or equivalent).

The purpose of the SPV shall be limited to:
- Acquiring and holding title to the Asset
- Entering into financing arrangements secured by the Asset
- Issuing digital tokens or certificates representing economic interests in the Asset
- Activities reasonably incidental to the foregoing

---

## RESOLUTION 2: ASSET CONTRIBUTION AUTHORIZED

The contribution and transfer of the Asset from the Company to the SPV is hereby approved.

The transfer shall be evidenced by an Asset Contribution Agreement in substantially the form reviewed by the Board.

In consideration for the Asset, the Company shall receive 100% of the membership interests or equity in the SPV.

---

## RESOLUTION 3: USE OF ASSET FOR FINANCING AUTHORIZED

The SPV is authorized to use the Asset as collateral or backing for:

(a) **Secured lending** – pledging the Asset to institutional lenders  
(b) **Tokenization** – issuing digital representations of economic interests  
(c) **Structured notes** – issuing debt instruments backed by the Asset  
(d) **Other financial arrangements** as approved by SPV management consistent with its purpose

---

## RESOLUTION 4: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

The Board hereby represents and warrants to the SPV, any future creditors, custodians, or investors:

1. The Company is the sole legal and beneficial owner of the Asset
2. The Asset is free and clear of all liens, security interests, encumbrances, and adverse claims
3. No litigation, arbitration, or governmental proceeding affects the Asset
4. The Company has full authority to transfer the Asset to the SPV
5. The Asset is not subject to export restrictions that would prevent lawful custody transfer
6. The appraisal report dated August 18, 2025 is accurate and complete to the best of the Company's knowledge

---
